City Green Space and Health Dashboard


In this website we explore how the acreage of city parks might affect that city's population health. We look to understand how city parks interact with obesity prevalence, self-reported mental health, and routine doctor checkups. We created a map that shows the location of the parks and the percent of the city that is city park land, bar charts that display the states with the 10 highest values in each area, and a scatter plot that shows the relationship between park acreage and the three factors. Please use the navigation are at the top of the screen to look at these visualizations!
